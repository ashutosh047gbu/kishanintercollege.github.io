About US

KISAN INTER C.PACHPOKHARI was established in 1967 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in BAGHAULI block of SANT KABIR NAGAR district of Uttar Pradesh. The school consists of Grades from 6 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road.
